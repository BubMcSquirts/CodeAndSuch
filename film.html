Nebula Heist

Beautiful Mind Media presents Nebula Heist, a story based in a new world where robots attempt to acquire a mysterious package.

This short film was written, directed, scored, animated, rendered and edited by Bub. Animation and rendering was done in the free and open source 3D software Blender. Music production and sound design was done in Ableton Live 11. Video editing was done in the video editor Davinci Resolve 18.5. Image editing was done in Adobe Photoshop.
